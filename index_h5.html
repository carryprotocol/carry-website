Revolutionizing economics for Gamers, Developers and Advertisers. Powering a new dynamic for On-Chain Games.
Made for
Mass Impact.
01.
Flexible
Ad Mechanism
Carry protocol offers fairminted "slots" as in-game assets designated for advertising, transforming advertisement spaces within games into tradable assets. This allows for direct placement of advertisements through these slots, eliminating intermediary costs.
02.
More Rewards for Players
and Developers
Offering a sustainable and healthier incentive system for all members of the gaming community, this approach ensures balanced rewards for both developers and gamers, funded by advertisers. It aids in preventing the decline of games often seen in the pay-to-earn (P2E) model.
03.
Conduct Analysis without
Central Platforms
Innovative methodology for Web3 Game analytics. Eliminating the understanding barrier between developers and gamers. Giving the ownership of data back to the community. 
04.
Easily Reaching Market
Equilibrium
Trading “slots” with Carry marketplace. Through auctioning, slots with better advertising performance will gain higher valuation, which can stimulate a fairer and more transparent environment for the gaming community.
Embedded Game Advertisements
With Carry protocol, Advertisements can be embedded within all the details of each game. Whether it's a billboard of a game, objects, animations or even pets.
Open Advertising Network
Advertisements are distributed in an open network fashion, connecting ad demands with requirements at minimal intermediary costs, thereby delivering substantial value to the entire gaming ecosystem. The money paid by advertisers can directly become incentives paid to gamers and developers.

Smooth Experience
Slot
In-game asset abstracted for advertisements to be placed. Carry protocol core concept for Web3 game advertisements. 
Easy Integration
Fairminting made easy to Developers and gamers for creating In-Game Assets, through the integration of the Carry SDK.
Diversity
Be a user and a creator at the same time. Carry protocol allows users to place their innovation everywhere.
Impact Generation
Creation by users can easily gain popularity through Carry Marketplace, also through the real gaming experience.
Effortlessly integrate slots into On-Chain games using the Carry Protocol SDK. These slots enable games to connect with open advertisement networks, providing a sustainable source of income for both gamers and developers. Carry mechanism allows users to create contents for advertising that the gaming industry has never seen before. And in the end, users will own what they create!
